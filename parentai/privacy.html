Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 17, 2026

1. What we collect

When you join our waitlist, we collect:

  • Your email address
  • The date and time of your signup

We do not collect names, phone numbers, payment information, or any other personal data at this stage.

2. How we use your data

Your email is used solely to:

  • Notify you when ParentAI launches
  • Send occasional product updates (no more than once a month)

3. Data storage

Waitlist emails are stored securely and accessible only to the ParentAI founding team. We do not share, sell, or transfer your data to third parties.

4. Your rights (GDPR)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation, you have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request deletion of your data at any time
  • Withdraw consent by unsubscribing

To exercise any of these rights, email us at privacy@parentai.app.

5. Analytics

We use Plausible Analytics, a privacy-friendly analytics tool that does not use cookies and does not collect personal data. It is fully GDPR compliant.

6. Cookies

ParentAI does not use cookies for tracking. We do not use any advertising cookies, third-party trackers, or fingerprinting technology. The only data stored locally in your browser is your waitlist signup status (via localStorage), which is used purely to prevent duplicate signups.

7. Room photos (future app)

When the full ParentAI app launches, room photos will be processed on-device using on-device AI models. Photos are never uploaded to our servers. Only anonymized cleanliness scores sync to your account.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as ParentAI evolves. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

9. Contact

Questions? Reach out at privacy@parentai.app.
